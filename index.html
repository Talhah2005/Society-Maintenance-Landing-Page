Designed for Everyone

Tailored experiences for every role in your community

👑 Admin Role

  • Full system control and superuser access
  • Add and manage team members
  • View and verify resident accounts
  • Track dues and generate reports
  • Set monthly maintenance fees
  • Monitor payment collections

👥 Team Member Role

  • View assigned houses for collection
  • Mark payments as collected
  • Automatic WhatsApp notifications
  • Simple, focused interface
  • Email verification required
  • Real-time status updates

🏠 Resident Role

  • View monthly maintenance bills
  • Check payment status and history
  • Register multiple vehicles
  • Email verification security
  • OTP-based password recovery
  • Instant payment confirmations
